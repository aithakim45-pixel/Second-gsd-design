ABOUT GULF STRUCTURAL DESIGN

The GCC's certified partner for advanced structural engineering software.

"We believe every structural engineer in the Gulf deserves access to world-class design tools, with local support."
Gulf Structural Design was founded to bring IDEA StatiCa's university-verified structural analysis technology to engineers across the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Qatar, Bahrain, and Oman. As the region's only certified reseller, we combine deep technical expertise with hands-on local support.
